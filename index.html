About Me

I have worked in the healthcare field all my life. From the age of 4, I knew I wanted to be a nurse. I have tried going back to school for many years, but life kept happening.

After about 20 years, I finally went back to school to become an LPN. While I was still attending nursing school, I also decided to learn how to code. It is exciting for me to be able to see something I have built. I've never felt very creative, but when I create applications and webpages, I feel very creative. I can let my creativity out.

I work as a private duty nurse. That is a nurse who works with disabled patients in their home. I enjoy my work. I have noticed that there is a need for a couple applications. One being a search for nurses to find new patients and for the patients to find the private duty nurses. The other being a documentation application. This will allow nurses to be able to document and save files electronically while also complying with HIPPA regulations. It has taken a lot of research to build this program, and I do need to do more research, but I am very excited to be bringing these applications to the field.

I like to keep myself busy. I am open to Full Stack Web Developer positions within the US.
